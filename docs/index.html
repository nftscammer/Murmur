Features

Built different.

Not a subscription. Not a cloud service. A proper Mac app that respects how you work.

⚡

Live word injection

Words appear as you speak — not after you stop. Murmur streams partial results directly into the focused app, replacing previous partials in real time.

🌍

Works everywhere

System-wide Accessibility API injection means Murmur types into any text field on your Mac — browsers, editors, Slack, Notes, Terminal, anything at all.

⌥

Global hotkey

Press ⌥Space to start, press again to stop. Or hold to record and release to transcribe. Set any key combo you like from inside the popover.

🎛

Three backends

Apple’s on-device recogniser for instant speed. Whisper (WhisperKit) for higher accuracy. Hybrid for the best of both: live Apple streaming, final Whisper pass.

🔀

Blind A/B testing

Enable A/B mode and Murmur randomly picks a backend each session. Rate each result and review cumulative stats to decide which engine suits your voice best.

🔒

Private by design

No account. No cloud sync. No telemetry. Audio never leaves your device. Sessions are stored in a local SQLite database and can be deleted any time.

How it works

From voice to text
in milliseconds.

1

Press ⌥Space anywhere

The global hotkey fires from any app. A subtle HUD appears at the bottom of your screen with a live waveform so you know it’s listening.

2

Your mic is tapped at 16 kHz

Murmur captures audio via AVAudioEngine, downsamples it to 16 kHz mono Float32 — the exact format both Apple Speech and Whisper expect.

3

Words stream as you speak

Apple’s SFSpeechRecognizer fires partial results every few hundred milliseconds. Each update is injected live via the Accessibility API, replacing the previous draft.

4

Final accuracy pass

When you stop, Whisper processes the full audio buffer with Core ML acceleration. Its final transcript replaces the live partial — speed and accuracy together.

5

Session logged locally

Duration, word count, latency, and transcript are saved to a local SQLite database. In A/B mode you can rate each result to build your own comparison dataset.

Engines

Three backends.
You choose.

Each backend makes a different accuracy/speed tradeoff. Use A/B mode to find which one suits your voice.

Apple
Fastest

Uses Apple’s built-in SFSpeechRecognizer. Partial results stream in real time. Runs fully on-device on Apple Silicon with near-zero latency to first word.

Hybrid
Recommended

Apple streams live words while you speak. When you stop, Whisper does a full-accuracy pass and replaces the draft. Best of both worlds for most people.

Whisper
Most accurate

OpenAI’s Whisper base.en model via WhisperKit, accelerated by Core ML. Slower to first word but significantly better accuracy for longer or complex speech.

Privacy

Your voice stays
on your Mac.

Murmur was built with a simple rule: nothing leaves your device without your knowledge.

✓

No audio uploads

Audio is processed entirely on-device — by Apple’s framework or by Whisper via Core ML. It is never sent to any external server.

✓

No account required

Download, install, use. No email, no sign-up, no login. The app functions entirely offline after the optional Whisper model download.

✓

No telemetry

Zero analytics, no crash reporting, no usage tracking of any kind. The app has no mechanism to contact any external server.

✓

Local storage only

Session logs live in ~/Library/Application Support/Murmur/ on your machine. Delete the folder to erase all history.

✓

Open source

The full source code is publicly available to inspect. Every claim on this page can be verified by reading the code.

✓

Transparent mic access

Murmur requests microphone permission only on first use. macOS shows the orange dot in the menu bar whenever the mic is active.
